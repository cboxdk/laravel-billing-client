# Cookbook

Practical, end-to-end recipes for the self-service [management client](../core-concepts/management-client.md).

- [Subscribe, change & cancel](subscribe-change-cancel.md) — the plan lifecycle a
  product app exposes to its users, including previewing a change before applying it.
- [Show usage](show-usage.md) — render the current period's per-meter usage and the
  invoice history.
- [Hosted checkout & portal](hosted-checkout.md) — take payment the low-effort way by
  redirecting to a billing-hosted checkout or self-service portal session (path A).
- [Embedded payment element](embedded-payment-element.md) — take payment in-page: create
  an intent, mount the gateway element client-side, handle SCA, and confirm by webhook
  (path B).
